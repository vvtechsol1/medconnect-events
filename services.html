FAQ

Common questions

How quickly can you turn around a proposal?
We return an outline plan and quote within 48 hours of your brief. Detailed programmes follow once we've aligned on scope, format and budget.
Do you work across both the UK and US?
Yes. We have dedicated delivery teams in London and Boston and run transatlantic programmes end to end, including virtual and hybrid formats that bridge time zones.
Can you manage CPD / CME accreditation?
Absolutely. We handle the accreditation process, track attendance and issue certificates, so your medical delegates earn the credits they need.
What size of event do you handle?
Anything from an intimate 30-person advisory board to a 2,000-delegate international congress — in person, virtual or hybrid.
