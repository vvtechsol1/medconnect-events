About Catalyst Corp

The event partner built for life sciences

For fourteen years we've brought sponsors, CROs, investigators and biotech innovators into one room — from first molecule to Phase IV. Compliant by design, memorable by intent, across the UK and United States.

Our mission

Connecting minds, advancing medicine

Great science moves faster when the right people are in the room. We exist to create those rooms — scientifically rigorous, fully compliant, and genuinely worth attending — so that research reaches patients sooner.

We're specialists, not generalists. Every planner on our team understands the difference between a CME session and a promotional one, why transfer-of-value reporting matters, and how to keep an investigator meeting on-protocol.

Our story

Fourteen years in the making

2012

Founded in London

Catalyst Corp opens its doors with a single focus: scientific events done properly, for pharma and medical clients.

2015

First flagship congress

We deliver our first 1,000-delegate clinical-trials congress, setting the template for compliant large-scale events.

2018

Boston office opens

A US base lets us run transatlantic programmes end-to-end, with local teams on both sides of the Atlantic.

2020

Catalyst Live launches

Our branded virtual & hybrid platform keeps the science moving when the world couldn't travel — and stays for good.

2026

320+ events and counting

Today we run 28+ events a year across oncology, clinical trials, drug discovery, regulatory and digital health.

Trust & compliance

Built for a regulated industry

Pharma and medical events carry obligations that generic organisers overlook. We handle them as standard:

  • ABPI & PhRMA code-aligned programme design
  • CPD / CME accreditation management
  • Transfer-of-value & disclosure reporting
  • GDPR & HIPAA-conscious data handling
  • Event code of conduct & accessibility as standard
